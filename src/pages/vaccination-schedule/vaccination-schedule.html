Vaccination Schedule

Keep up to date with the vaccines for you and your baby

Correct as of 1st April 2016

Before birth, pregnancy vaccinations

Recommended vaccination When Flu vaccine The flu vaccine can be administered safely at any stage of pregnancy. It is offered during the flu season from September onwards. Whooping cough (pertussis) vaccine From 16 weeks of pregnancy ideally up to 32 weeks. If you miss the vaccine you can still have it up to when you give birth but your baby is less likely to get protection after 32 weeks.
